# Global DSGE Solutions

## Models in this section
Nonlinear, global solutions for DSGE models using projection methods
instead of local perturbation (linearization).

## Common patterns
- Value function iteration on grids (tensor-product or adaptive sparse)
- Policy function interpolation
- Simulation of nonlinear dynamics
- Comparison with linearized (Dynare) solutions

## Key economics
- RBC with nonlinear policy rules (precautionary behavior)
- Occasionally binding constraints (irreversible investment, ZLB)
- Capital taxation with nonlinear effects
- Why global solutions matter: perturbation misses asymmetries and risk premia
