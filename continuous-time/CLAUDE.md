# Continuous-Time Models

## Models in this section
Equilibrium models in continuous time using HJB equations,
Kolmogorov Forward Equations, and finite difference methods.

## Common patterns
- Hamilton-Jacobi-Bellman (HJB) equation with upwind finite differences
- Kolmogorov Forward Equation (KFE) for stationary distributions
- Sparse matrix construction for transition dynamics
- Bisection on prices for general equilibrium

## Key economics
- Huggett (1993): incomplete markets with idiosyncratic risk
- Consumption-savings in continuous time
- Interest rate determination in general equilibrium
